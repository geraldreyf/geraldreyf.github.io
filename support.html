Frequently asked

Is my data backed up anywhere?
No — everything you enter in Jubilee stays only on your device. If you delete the app or lose your device, that data can't be recovered. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Is Jubilee really free?
Yes. No ads, no subscriptions, no debt limit. There's an optional "Buy Me a Coffee" link in Settings if you'd like to support development, but nothing in the app is ever paywalled.
